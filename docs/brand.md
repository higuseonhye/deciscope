# Deciscope — Brand Document

---

## 🧠 Name

**Deciscope**

---

## 🔥 Tagline

**Don’t just decide. Understand.**

---

### Alternatives

- See inside every decision  
- AI decisions you can actually trust  

---

## 🧭 Category

**Trustable Decision System**

---

## 💡 One-liner

Deciscope helps you understand, diagnose, and improve decisions made by AI and humans.

---

## 🧠 Core Concept

Deciscope is not an AI that generates answers.

It is a system that:
- makes decisions
- explains why
- detects what’s wrong
- shows how to fix it

---

## 🧬 Core Structure

Decision → Reasoning → Diagnosis → Prescription → System MRI

---

## 🧬 Core Metaphor

**MRI for decisions**

Deciscope scans:
- how a decision was made  
- where it breaks  
- what needs to change  

---

## ❌ What Deciscope is NOT

- ChatGPT  
- Copilot  
- simple AI tool  
- dashboard  

---

## ✅ What Deciscope IS

- decision infrastructure  
- trust layer for AI  
- diagnosis engine  
- reasoning scanner  

---

## 🎯 Target Users

- AI product builders  
- PMs / Founders  
- Agent developers  
- VCs / strategists  

---

## 🚀 Core Use Cases

- failure analysis
