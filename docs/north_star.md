# Deciscope — North Star Document

---

## 🔥 Product Definition

**Deciscope is a Trustable Decision System.**

It helps users:
- make decisions
- understand why
- detect what’s wrong
- know how to improve

---

## 🧠 Problem

### Surface Problems

- AI provides answers, but users cannot trust them
- Reasoning exists, but cannot be verified
- When failures happen, root causes are unclear
- No structured way to improve decisions

---

### Core Problem

> AI systems are increasingly used, but their internal decision-making remains opaque and untrustworthy.

---

## 🎯 What We Build

Deciscope is NOT:
- a chatbot
- a copilot
- a generic AI tool

Deciscope IS:
- a decision system
- a diagnosis engine
- a trust layer for AI-driven decisions

---

## 🧬 Core Capabilities

### 1. Final Decision

Provide a clear outcome:
- Go / No-Go / Pivot / Wait
- Confidence level
- Risk level

---

### 2. Reasoning

- concise explanation of the decision
- optional deep reasoning trace

---

### 3. Diagnosis

Identify structural issues such as:
- faulty assumptions
- missing data
- logical inconsistencies
- risk factors

---

### 4. Prescription

Provide actionable next steps:
- what to fix
- what to test
- what data to collect

---

### 5. System MRI (Key Differentiator)

> Analyze how the decision was produced, not just the result.

Includes:
- LLM issues
- prompt design issues
- agent workflow issues
- data pipeline issues

---

## 🧱 Output Structure

1. Final Decision  
2. Reasoning  
3. Diagnosis  
4. Prescription  
5. System MRI  
6. Deep Thinking (optional)  

---

## 🧭 UX Principles

### 1. Decision First

Users come for answers.  
Always present the final decision upfront.

---

### 2. Progressive Disclosure

- Show summary first
- Allow users to expand details on demand

---

### 3. Diagnosis → Action

Every identified problem must be paired with a clear solution.
